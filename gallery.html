  • Bill is a Belfast, Ireland native with many years of hitchhiking and medical experience. He was volunteering at a hospital in Antigua and is an amazing conversationalist. He is passionate about many things, especially about the unfortunate, misleading media portrayals of many countries. I will never forget when he said, "meeting you two, I can die a little happier." He was pleased that although we come from a different generation, we also refuse to accept media's limited portrayal of a place that is not representative of a whole group of people (Ex. Although Mexico is portrayed as dangerous, we had only wonderful encounters with people in Mexico and found cycling to be even safer than in parts of the U.S. Many travelers and cyclist, who don't go seeking drugs, say the same.) So glad we met Bill!

  • Jose and Mary have a home along a popular travel route in El Salvador. They host hundreds of bicycle tourists each year, sometimes up to 10 at once (there were 3 others when we arrived!) Having grown up in Quebec, Jose speaks fluent French and bike toured form Quebec--> New York --> LA (over the Rockies) --> baja & Mexico --> El Salvador, where he met his wife. Between his travels and his love for hosting travelers, he had the best stories. This man is a legend. AND he gave us the keys to his car to check out all the nearby beaches for a day! The best.

  • We took the dirt road detour to stay at a Warmshowers host on Playa Gigante. John (the coolest person ever) owns a hostel on this paradise beach and having done a year solo journey around Asia, has a warm spot for bicycle travelers. He built this tree-house looking home on the beach next to his hostel and opens up the downstairs for volunteers and bicycle tourists. Our rest day went as followed: - Invite dot join beach yoga class with hostel staff (how they begin each day) -Took the horses for a walk on the beach -Invited to go 4 hours sailing around the bay -Nap in a hammock -Sunset ocean swim I had to pinch myself multiple times while I was there. A free paradise vacation?
