  • While the new highways in Mexico have smooth, huge shoulders perfect for cycling, we do enjoy making detours that take us through many small towns. One day at sundown we came across Pretoria. We passed Arturo (left) and asked him where a safe place to camp would be. He insisted we come stay with him and his family and let us know there were a few people still praying at his home (people are very devout here so we thought nothing of it). Turns out they were having a funeral for his mother, who seemed like a wonderful woman. Should have been awkward, right? This family welcomed us, shared stories about their life and asked about ours, and fed us delicious home cooked meals. What a family!
